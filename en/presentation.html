About

For over 30 years, Poli Bijoux has embodied polishing excellence in high jewelry.
As a passionate artisan, I work with the most prestigious houses on every stage of creation. I bring constant attention to detail and a bespoke approach, faithful to the identity of each brand.

Why this site?

This site was born from a desire to pass on and highlight a little-known skill that has become a rare profession: polishing.
With the intention of leaving a legacy and reaching future artisans.
